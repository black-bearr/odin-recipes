Lasagna

Description

This is one of the most widely reviewed and beloved lasagna recipes in existance on the internet today, with not a single one star review and that's including operator error. Submitted by a Mr. John Chandler over twenty years ago, he had no idea just how popular it would become. Unfortunately the creator of it suddenly passed away in 2022, at the age of 53, but his name is cemented in culinary history as the best lasagna in the world.

The preparation of the lasagna is the most time consuming part as the cook time is approx. 30-45 minutes depending on how thick you make the dish. The preparation and putting together of the lasagna will take around an hour, again depending on the thickness of your desired lasagna; you will also cook the lasagna for half the time covered with a piece of foil, and the other half without.

Ingredients

  • Meat: This super meaty lasagna has sweet italian sausage and lean ground beef.
  • Lasagna Noodles: Use store-bought or homemade noodles.
  • Onions and Garlic: An onion and two cloves of garlic are cooked with the meats to add tons of flavors.
  • Tomato products: You'll need a can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste.
  • Sugar: Two tablespoons of white sugar add subtle sweetness and enhance the flavor of the sauce.
  • Seasonings: This lasagna recipe is flavored with fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Cheese: Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese make this lasagna extra decadent.
  • Egg: An egg helps bind the ricotta so it doesn't ooze out of the lasagna when you cut into it.
