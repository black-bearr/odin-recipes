Cheese Burgers

Description

Your new favorite cheeseburger recipe! There's nothing like a juicy cheeseburger with fresh crisp toppings on a toasted bun smothered with an irresistible burger sauce. We are sharing our best tips for making the perfect resturaunt-style burger with the juiciest patty you'll ever sink your teeth into.

We ove burger recipes from our cheeseburger sliders to hamburger soup but we make these classic burgers most often; and we're sharing the recipe with you here below. Pair these juicy hamburgers with grilled corn and air fryer french fries for the perfect cookout menu.

Ingredients

  • 80/20 Ground Beef Chuck
  • Fine Sea Salt
  • Freshly Ground Black Pepper
  • Burger Buns
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • A Tomato, sliced
  • Leaf Lettuce
  • A medium Red Onion Sliced Into Rings
  • Dill Pickle Coins
  1. Form the burger into a patty shape 1" wider than the bun because they'll shrink
  2. Make an indentation in the center to prevent it from plumping up in the middle
  3. Place patties in a saute pan or on a grill, let patties brown and sear well (3-5 minutes) before flipping, do not press down on the burger.
  4. Add cheese in last few minutes.
  5. Slice all produce into desired shape and size.
  6. Apply a generous amount of sauce of your choice to both buns.
  7. Place pickles, lettuce, tomatoes, and red onion onto the bottom bun.
  8. Place cheesy burger patty over the veggies and cover with the top bun.
  9. Enjoy!
